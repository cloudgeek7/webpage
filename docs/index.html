Cloud Implementation

Cloud Consult

Every company needs to look into the future requirements of IT and ongoing demand of Cloud services.

Focusing on the people, processes, systems, data and security that underpin your business, we work with you to understand how changes to your infrastructure and your technology could enable you to grow, improve your margins, or overcome specific business challenges. They could be application specific or infrastructure related.

Our consulting work is related to a need or a will to move to cloud, but due to a lack of clarity on how to do it. Through our consultation and analysis, we assess your requirements against your business strategy and your resources, making recommendations on how to move to Cloud in the most cost effective way.

Cloud Plan

Once you decide in principle to migrate an application or a component of your infrastructure to the cloud, you need to figure out what it will cost and the best way to do it. By modelling variations of your future ecosystem, we make sure you get the best value and that you don’t get any expensive surprises. Our Cloud Strategy gives you the assurance that each phase of your transformation is planned, costed and achievable.

A fully-costed action plan for transforming systems and applications. Reduced risk and cost for migrating applications. CIOs and teams that manage application portfolios. Application owners who manage strategic roadmaps. Transformation specialists planning complex migrations.

Cloud Cost Optimisation

Do your cloud bills shock you - either in real dollar terms, or with their wild unpredictability? Are engineering and finance talking past each other, while the cloud bill continues to grow? Are you concerned that your bill increases more rapidly than your user growth? We can help.

Moving to the cloud brought the promise of reducing your technology costs by eliminating large capital expenses and giving you the ability to pay for only the resources and power you actually need. But for many companies, the economic reality of the cloud is that costs steadily rise over time, without clear gains in performance, flexibility, or user experience. Reducing costs becomes an imperative, but without clear insight into what drives your spend, strategic cost reduction is impossible.

Docker

Dockerisation enables teams to optimise their resources utilisation, increases their agility, and helps them achieve faster and less painful deployments.

Containerisation

Containerisation is an OS-level virtualisation which allows you to utilise your hardware/VMs better. Containers share the host OS kernel instead of carrying their own. This makes them lightweight and faster to boot. They also use less resources than VMs.


Traditionally, deploying an application means ordering a physical/virtual server, provisioning it with the right dependencies, and then installing the application on it. Such a process can take anywhere from hours to days. Containerisation is meant to streamline this process by making it simpler and faster.
